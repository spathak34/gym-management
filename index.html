Personal Training

A personal trainer is an individual certified to have a varying degree of knowledge of general fitness involved in exercise prescription and instruction.

Cardio Training

Cardio exercise is any exercise that raises your heart rate. Face it our bodies were made to move. And we all know that to keep our muscles in shape we need move them.

Freestyle Training

Freestyle Training class is a social, free-flowing style of fitness session. It enables members to train across multiple exercise.

Sport Nutrition

Sports Nutrition focuses its studies on the type, as well as the quantity of fluids and food taken by an athlete.

Functional Training

Functional training has its origins in rehabilitation. Physical and occupational therapists and chiropractors often use this approach to retrain patients with movement disorders.

Crossfit

A high-intensity fitness programme incorporating elements from several sports and types of exercise.
